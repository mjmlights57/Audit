# BGE Terms PDF and Customer Signature

## Auditor workflow

1. Open the appointment.
2. Complete Confirm Address & Account Number, photos, and equipment sections.
3. Open **Customer T & C**.
4. Confirm the authorized representative name.
5. Have the customer accept the Terms and Conditions.
6. Have the customer sign with a finger or Apple Pencil.
7. Save the section. The signature date is recorded automatically.
8. Select **Submit Audit & Share BGE PDF + CSV**.
9. The app fills the attached BGE Terms and Conditions & Customer Acknowledgement PDF and opens the iPad Share Sheet.

## Fields filled on page 3

- Project Name: appointment/customer name
- BGE Electric Account Number: confirmed utility account number
- Street Address, City, State, and Zip: appointment address
- Authorized Representative: customer signer name
- Title, email, and phone: appointment/customer data when available
- Payment to: Service Provider (already selected in the template)
- Rebate Assignee Company/Entity Name: EWPros
- Rebate Assignee Address: the EWPros address already printed in the template
- Authorized Representative Signature: customer signature from the app
- Date: date the signature is saved

Pages 1 and 2 of the supplied BGE document remain unchanged.
